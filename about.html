ABOUT

Treyvon Spiva is a Chicago-based editor dedicated to crafting compelling stories through the art of editing. His passion for film began at a young age, when movies became both an escape and a revelation — showing him the emotional power of filmmaking. After graduating Columbia College Chicago, Treyvon now works as an Assistant Editor at Picture North. There, he contributes to national campaigns for brands like Ford, Build-A-Bear, and Victoria Beer, continuing to refine his style.
