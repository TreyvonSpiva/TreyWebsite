ABOUT

Treyvon Spiva is a Chicago-based editor dedicated to crafting compelling stories through the art of editing. His passion began at a young age, when film became an escape from the world around him. He quickly fell in love with the idea of creating work that people can truly connect with. He started by editing animated music videos and YouTube content, using those early projects to sharpen his technical skills. Treyvon went on to earn a degree in Post Production from Columbia College Chicago. Today, he works as an Assistant Editor at Picture North, where he contributes to national campaigns for brands such as Ford, Build-A-Bear, and Victoria Beer.
