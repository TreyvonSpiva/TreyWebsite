ABOUT

Treyvon Spiva is a Chicago-based editor working across narrative and branded storytelling. His passion for editing began after watching Scott Pilgrim vs. the World — a film that revealed to him that editing doesn’t always have to be invisible; it can be expressive, rhythmic, and intentionally seen. That realization shaped his approach. Treyvon is drawn to tension, momentum, and structural precision — crafting work where pacing becomes part of the storytelling itself. He is currently an Assistant Editor at Picture North, continuing to refine his voice across commercial and narrative projects.
